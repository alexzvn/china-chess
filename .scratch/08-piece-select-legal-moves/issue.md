## What to build

Players can click their own pieces to select them. The selected piece gets a visual highlight (golden border/glow). The engine computes legal moves for the selected piece, and the board displays them: small filled circles on empty destination squares, rings around capturable enemy pieces. Clicking a square that is not a legal move deselects the piece. Clicking another own piece re-selects to that piece. The legal moves are computed by the pure engine module. This slice is frontend-only with a mock board state — no server round-trip yet.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Clicking own piece (same color as current turn) selects it — golden border/glow appears
- [ ] Legal destination squares show: small filled dots (empty squares) and rings (capture squares)
- [ ] Clicking a non-legal square deselects the piece, removing all highlights
- [ ] Clicking another own piece re-selects (previous highlight removed, new piece highlighted with its legal moves)
- [ ] Legal moves are computed by calling the engine's `getLegalMoves()` function
- [ ] Clicking an opponent's piece while none is selected does nothing
- [ ] Legal move dots/rings scale with the responsive board
- [ ] Component test: clicking a 兵 shows legal forward/sideways moves; clicking 車 shows full orthogonal slides
